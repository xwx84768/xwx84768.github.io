  <p>We propose <strong>ADAS</strong>, a detection method that exploits the sensitivity disparity between clean and adversarial samples under re-attacks. ADAS achieves strong robustness to minimal-perturbation attacks and shows good generalization to unseen adversarial methods across multiple datasets and architectures.</p>
